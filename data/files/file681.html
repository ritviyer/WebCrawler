He said: "He [Park] will be playing many games in the Premier League, he will be able to grow into a better player. It might be helpful for the national team."

There were immediate signs, however, that Park had problems. Although he scored the winner in a 2-1 League Cup win over Bolton Wanderers, his Champions League debut for his new club in November 2011 proved to be a disaster.
